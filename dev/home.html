MEET THE AUTHOR

Abbie was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah where she attended the University of Utah. She earned a degree in accounting, a Masters of Accountancy and has a CPA license. And although she excelled in her academic pursuits, her real passion has always been for the arts. Abbie is a gifted musician, dancer, performer, artist and now, author. Her desire to write was inspired by her beautiful family who make her feel like she can accomplish anything. Abbie hopes to bring joy and happiness through her fantastical stories.

“This book is fantastic!


When it comes to children’s books, I’m pretty picky—and this one checks all the boxes:

  1. Clever, flowing rhymes – The rhyming is smooth and creative, making it a joy to read aloud.
  2. Fun, expressive illustrations – Every page is full of personality.
  3. A wonderful message – Most importantly, it teaches the value of confidence and embracing who you are. That’s such an important lesson that I love sharing with my kids.

I’ve thoroughly enjoyed reading this book with my family and would highly recommend it to anyone looking for a meaningful, engaging children’s story.”
