MEET THE AUTHOR

Abbie was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah where she attended the University of Utah. She earned a degree in accounting, a Masters of Accountancy and has a CPA license. And although she excelled in her academic pursuits, her real passion has always been for the arts. Abie is a gifted musician, dancer, performer, artist and now, author. Her desire to write was inspired by her beautiful family, who make her feel like she can accomplish anything. Abbie hopes to bring joy and happiness through her fantastical stories.
